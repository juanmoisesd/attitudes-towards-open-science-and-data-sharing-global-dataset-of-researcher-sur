# Business Continuity Plan

## Objective
To ensure the long-term availability of the dataset even if GitHub or the primary institution becomes unavailable.

## Strategy
1. **Redundancy:** Data is mirrored on GitHub and Zenodo.
2. **Offline Backups:** Quarterly backups maintained by the Project Lead.
3. **Legacy:** In the event the Project Lead can no longer maintain the repository, maintenance will transition to the Universidad Internacional de La Rioja (UNIR) data repository team.
