# Provenance

This dataset follows the W3C PROV-O standard for documenting its lineage.

## Data Life Cycle
1. **Source Collection:** Raw survey data collected from international repositories.
2. **Extraction:** Data extracted from various formats.
3. **Cleaning:** Standardization of country names and variable normalization.
4. **Aggregation:** Compilation into the final longitudinal dataset (2018-2024).
5. **Validation:** Automated schema and integrity checks.
