Answering Key Questions

Using data evidence to address common inquiries in the Open Science community.

Q1

Does institutional prestige correlate with data sharing?

Our cross-analysis suggests that researchers from highly ranked institutions (Top 100 Global) are 12% more likely to use public repositories (Zenodo/Figshare), but 8% less likely to share raw data before formal publication compared to researchers in mid-tier institutions.
Evidence: Dataset Dimension 3
Q2

What is the most effective incentive for OS?

Data shows that "Formal recognition in career progression" is ranked #1 (selected by 74% of respondents) as the primary driver, significantly outperforming "Direct financial rewards" (only 12%).
Evidence: Survey Variable 'Incentive_Rank'
Q3

How has the COVID-19 pandemic affected these trends?

There was a measurable "pandemic spike" in 2020-2021, where adoption of OS practices accelerated by 2.4x the historical trend rate. This acceleration has slightly plateaued in 2023-2024 but remains significantly above pre-2020 levels.
Evidence: Longitudinal Analysis (2018-2024)
