# Risk Register

| Risk | Impact | Probability | Mitigation Strategy |
| ---- | ------ | ----------- | ------------------- |
| Data Obsolescence | High | Medium | Annual updates from primary sources. |
| Loss of Funding/Support | Medium | Low | Long-term preservation in Zenodo. |
| Security Breach | High | Low | Implementation of SLSA and cryptographic signing. |
| Low Adoption | Low | Medium | Enhanced visualization and microsite promotion. |
