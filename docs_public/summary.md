## What is this dataset?

This dataset compiles quantitative data from international surveys (2018-2024) on attitudes of academic researchers towards open science and data sharing. Includes five key analytical dimensions: (1) Willingness to share data by discipline; (2) Repos

## Why does it matter?

This dataset contributes to open science by making research data freely available for reuse, analysis, and citation.

## Who can use it?

- Researchers and academics
- Policy makers
- Journalists and science communicators
- Students and educators
- General public

## How to use it?

1. Download the data from [GitHub](https://github.com/juanmoisesd/attitudes-towards-open-science-and-data-sharing-global-dataset-of-researcher-sur)
2. Open with Excel, R, Python or any data analysis tool
3. Cite using the CITATION.cff or CITATION.bib files
