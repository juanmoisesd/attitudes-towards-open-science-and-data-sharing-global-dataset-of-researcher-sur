# Data Protection Statement

The data presented in this repository is derived from aggregated results of international surveys.

## Personal Data
- No personally identifiable information (PII) is contained within this dataset.
- All individual responses have been anonymized and aggregated by the original survey providers before being compiled into this dataset.

## Compliance
- This dataset complies with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) as it does not process personal data of living individuals.
- Data collection and aggregation methods were designed to ensure the privacy and confidentiality of survey participants.
